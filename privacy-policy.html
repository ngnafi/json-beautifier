Privacy Policy

At JSON Beautifier Online, accessible from https://json-beautifier-online.netlify.app, the privacy of our visitors is one of our main priorities. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that are collected and recorded by JSON Beautifier Online and how we use it.

Information We Collect

JSON Beautifier Online does not collect any personal information directly from users. All JSON data entered into the tool is processed locally in your browser and is not stored or transmitted to any server.

Log Files

JSON Beautifier Online follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files include internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks.
